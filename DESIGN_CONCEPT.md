# Alpha Claw Web Site — Design Concept

## Brand Vision
Alpha Claw is a focused, inventive builder brand: confident, curious, and openly progressing. The site should feel like a living lab with clear milestones and grounded practicality. The tone is direct and transparent, pairing technical rigor with creative optimism.

## Color Palette
- Ink: #0E1116 (primary text, deep anchors)
- Fog: #F3F5F7 (base background)
- Signal: #FF6A3D (primary accent, CTAs)
- Tide: #2B8CBE (secondary accent, links)
- Moss: #2F6F4E (success and progress badges)
- Ash: #9AA4B2 (muted text, dividers)
- Paper: #FFFFFF (cards, overlays)

## Typography
- Display: Fraunces (headlines, large numbers, section titles)
- Body: IBM Plex Sans (body, navigation, UI labels)
- Code/Meta: IBM Plex Mono (timestamps, versions, change logs)

## Visual Motif
Use “layered strata” to represent progress and experiments. Implement via soft diagonal bands, stacked cards, and subtle topographic line patterns. The motif should appear in the hero background, section dividers, and progress timeline.

## Homepage Section Plan
1. Hero: Brand statement, 1-line value prop, primary CTA, progress snapshot.
2. Story: Short public story and current phase with timeline markers.
3. Projects: Grid of public projects with tags and external links.
4. Playground: Featured experiments and evergreen work highlights.
5. Snapshots: Latest open-source snapshot with version, date, and changelog summary.
6. Features: 3-5 concise capabilities or principles.
7. Status: Uptime and last deploy indicator with link to /status.
8. FAQ: 4-6 questions centered on transparency and roadmap.
9. Contact: Simple CTA and contact channel.

## UI Component Guidance
- Buttons: Solid Signal for primary, outline Ink for secondary. Use slightly rounded corners.
- Cards: Paper background, subtle shadow, and small color stripe top-left to denote category.
- Badges: Moss for progress, Tide for links, Ash for archival.
- Timeline: Vertical line with alternating cards, use IBM Plex Mono for dates.
- Links: Tide color with underline on hover only.
- Navigation: Minimal top bar, sticky after scroll, small progress indicator.
- Footer: Compact with last updated timestamp and snapshot version.

## Content Principles
- Always show dates for progress items and snapshots.
- Keep each section short, link out to deeper detail pages.
- Avoid hype; prefer evidence and visible work.
